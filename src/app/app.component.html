Technology for technologists

We've exposed a simple but secured REST API for your technologists to send a simplified data object to be collected and then visualized. Our documentation is built for programmatic interactions, and our SDKs will be released soon!

Information for all

Technical and non-technical staff need data to make the best decisions. Our dashboard is built to inform all users to enable proper prioritization based off of performance.

Providing insights into performance

acumulus was born out of necessity. Seeing a lack of reporting tools around application performance, we decided to provide the market with a simple tool for developers to send data and a dashboard that was easy for non-technologist to view that data.

Grainularity as you need it

Accumulated metrics for visualization is available in any level of grainularity that you need. Second/second, minute/minute, day/day.

Performance insights

Visualize your distributed application's metrics. Know when dependent services are called, how long it takes, and view those metrics over time.

Endless specificity

With analytics and insights into your application's performance over time, you can know where in your application needs the most attention.
